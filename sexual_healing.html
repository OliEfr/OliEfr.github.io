Scores and Tabs for Sexual Healing by Marvin Gaye (1982)

Picking up the guitar and jamming along Sexual Healing is not far fetched. It's got that characteristic funky-rhythmic groove. Finding the chords for the song is easy, however, I could not find any tabs or sheet music for the lead and rhythm guitars. Also, it is worth noting that the YouTube and Spotify version of the song are slightly different. For both it is quite difficult to identify the notes played by the guitars from the rest of the song as just a lot is going on. I uploaded all sheet music here.
