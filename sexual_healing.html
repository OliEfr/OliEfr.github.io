Sexual Healing, Guitar #1 and #2

So, picking up the guitar and jamming along Sexual Healing, by Marvin Gaye (1982) is not far fetched. It's really got that characteristic funky-rhythmic groove that's nice to play along. Finding the chords for the song on the usual platforms was easy, however, I could find any tabs or sheet music for the lead and rhythm guitar on the internet. I actually also just found out that the YouTube and Spotify version of the song are different. This YouTube Video does great job playing along the YouTube version. However, it is still difficult to separate the guitars from the rest of the song as a lot is going on in the rhythm group.

Sheets created with MuseScore. PS: I now found some good piano scores on musescore for the Spotify version, I think.
